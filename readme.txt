== Description ==


We aim to caress your auditory receptors, bringing you to a harmonized climax, leaving you wanting more. Allow us to pound your ears with our energetic reverberation – please bring along a friend for even more fun!

Enable your visitors to listen to 24/7 Rock and Pop music, from your Sidebar. Increase visitor retention, and possibly advertising revenue.


== Installation ==


After you install and activate the plug-in WordPress Admin panel, go to:
Appearance > Widgets

Drag the'Rock & Pop Radio' component to the section you want to insert the side menu and save.

1. Upload the rockpopradio folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Place the widget in your side bar.  Your sidebar must have a minimum width of 200px.
